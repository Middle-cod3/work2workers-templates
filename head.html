Frequently Asked Questions
How to book a service on NoBroker?
Just click on the service required by you, see the prices and fill some basic contact details to schedule the service.
Who is going to fulfill the service?
We will assign a NoBroker Partner to complete your service at your preferred time slot.
Who is a NoBroker Partner?
NoBroker Partners are selected individuals / companies who meet our stringent criteria for delivering quality home services.
What all services are available on NoBroker?
We provide services like Home Painting, Home Cleaning, Electrician, Plumbing, Carpentry.
How are NoBroker services different from other online portals?
Other online portals merely put you in touch with the service providers leaving the service quality upto individual vendors. On the other hand, all NoBroker services are owned and fulfilled by NoBroker Partners who adhere to a strict quality criteria.
